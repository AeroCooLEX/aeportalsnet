Game Version: v1.22.0-rc.2 (Unstable)

Zstd Version: 1.5.5
Operating System: Linux (Linux Mint 22.1) [Kernel 6.8.0.101]
Simple portals. which you can create yourself. Facilitates travel around the world of vintage story.
Functional:
- not a difficult craft;
- the ability to use on multi-user servers;
- protecting the portal from destruction by other players (except the administration);
- displaying the name of the portal owner's player;
- the ability to give names to portals (PCM by portal);
- displaying the name of the portal;
- no one except the owner of the portal will be able to teleport to your portal points.;
- If another player uses your portal block and renames it, the mod will consider this block to be the portal of both players, meaning both players will use the same block
- and will not lose the teleportation point.;
- each player has their own network of portals;
- a selection window with several pages of your portals;
- the ability to teleport from any portal you have created to any of your other portals;
- the ability to move to another location of your portal;
